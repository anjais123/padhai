Studots

Studots is combination of two words. Student and dots which means Students who connect the dots. This idea of Studots came to our mind while we got stuck in the world’s largest lockdown due to COVID-19. lives came to a standstill and uncertainty which engulfed the very foundational part of childhood, schools. We decided to contribute our bit by providing quality education.
